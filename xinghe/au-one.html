Stock Name: XINGHEZHONGGONG(Stock Code: 430084).

We are committed to providing technical solutionfor metal strip surface processing, combining R&D, design, manufacturing, installing,commissioning as well as technical service. We have a modern technical research manufacturing facility and have gained 13 National Invention Patents, 42 new and practical technical patents and many software copyright certificates.

We can provide the following metal process lines and corresponding equipments: Home Appliance Color Coating Line, Construction Color Coaring Line, Hot-dip Galvanizing/Galvalume line, Electrolytic Tinning Line, Electrolytic Degreasing line, Reversible Cold Rolling Mill, Pickling Line andAcid Regeneration Plant(ARP).

SRI has several quality management systems approved by internationally recognized organizations such as EU CE Certificates, and has been accredited ISO90001, ISO14000, GB/T28001 approved intended for environmental, health, safety awareness. We have always been folowing the quality guaidlines of technial innovation, exquisite management, honest service and customer first to create the maximum value for our clients. With good reputation and performance.we have many overseas customers in Portugal, Russia, Turkey, Iran, India, Bengal, Indonesia and Vietnam and so on.
