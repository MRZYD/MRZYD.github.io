  • Laminating Steel Line

Laminatin steel line is a complete set of manufacturing equipments that help to form a multilayer compound polyester film on the surface of steel plate or chromate plate throught heating with high temperature or extrusion or adhesion after degreasing treatment to form compound material with both of plastic film and metallic plate features.

\ Process Parameter

The lamingating equipments adopt overseas advanced ultrahigh frequency induction heating furnace to realize fast and uniform plate heating. With temperature detection units, the equipments make automatic temperature control of plates possible. We have very strong R&D capabilities of both hot and cold lamination in CCL processes. Also, in technological analysis and comparison, especially in film and coating performances, we have very rich experience.
